Software Creation

We will help you build well-crafted software that is valuable to your business.

Our craftsmen have honed their skills in technologies and practices so that they deliver with quality on time. we can engage with you according to your needs.

Embedded Craftsmen

Our craftsmen can enhance your teams to provide expertise in specific technologies and expertise. They can also form the core of your teams to help you instil XP practices and Software Craftsmanship values from the outset.

Self-contained Teams

Our craftsmen are equipped to cover all the roles required for a fully functioning Agile teams. We have experts in; UX and UI design, Front End and Back End Development, DevOps and Testing. All our craftsmen maintain an innate understanding of business analysis and work with the product owner to ensure that we constantly deliver value.

We will provide fully functional teams to help you get started quick and delivery you project in the most effective manner.

Continuous Delivery Pipeline

We are experts in technologies that are enablers for Continuous Delivery. We also understand how the business needs to be aligned to realise the full benefits of this approach. We are also programatic and understand that certain organisational constrains cannot change and help you tailor the pipeline to work around these constraints.

UX & UI Design

We have expertise in designing visual identity and user experiences and understand how this efforts fits best in an Agile environment. We will help you define the brand identity for your project and create UX and UI designs as part of your software development effort.

Expert Advice

You can draw from our expertise to fast track your initiatives.

We can help you start a new project, instil XP practices or Devops culture into your existing teams, plan your architectural evolution or adopt a new technology.

We are available to run workshops and engage with you on a short-term basis to ensure that you put your best foot forward for your next initiative.

Enterprise Architecture

Our Craftsmen have a wealth of expertise and experiences in dealing with large scale systems in finance, media and government.

We will review your approach and help you make the most effective decisions for the future.

Software Architecture & Design

We will review your current architecture and help you create a risk-based view of your software solution. We can advise on the best way to evolve your architecture to reduce those risks.

If you are looking to evolve your system into microservices or create a microservices based architecture from scratch, we will help you understand the best approach according to your needs.

Continuous Delivery

We are experts in creating continuous delivery pipelines. We understand that it is not only a technical challenge but also an organisational one. We help your customers evolve their approach towards continuous delivery aligning their business, teams and technology.

Extreme Programming Practices

XP practices are core to every Craftsman in Codurance. We love to coach and mentor our customers in adopting XP practices. We advise you on how to adopt the practices to bring about positive change in your teams.

Training & Coaching

We provide a range of training opportunities that typically run for 2 days.

Our training courses help teams get better at Test Driven Development, Continuous Integration and Deployment, Domain-Driven Design, Clean Code and SOLID principles, Behaviour Driven Development and many other technical practices.

We help teams in any phase of a project where they may benefit from focused training.
