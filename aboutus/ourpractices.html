HOW WE WORK

Software Craftsmanship

Software Craftsmanship is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that the value of craftsmanship is in continuously delivering value to the business with inherent quality. We understand that quality should not be a premium. We hone our skills through continuous practice. We constantly learn, teach and mentor in our team, our company, and in the wider software development industry.

Agile and Lean Software Development

We believe that a good process constantly delivers incremental value to the business and provides a short feedback cycle. The short feedback allows us to inspect, learn, and adapt so that we are constantly improve the way we deliver. Continuous Delivery

We understand that Continuous Delivery requires the whole organisation be aligned in order to deliver value to the business in frequent and small iterations. We are experts in release automation and setting up continuous delivery pipelines. Releasing often is easy but releasing defect free features that are aligned to the business, is not easy. We achieve continuous delivery through close attention to business needs, software quality, and Extreme Programming practices.

Extreme Programming

Practices such as TDD, Pair Programming, Continuous Integration and Shared Ownership are core to every craftsman at Codurance. This fits very well with our craftsmanship values. We are renowned experts in these practices and use it with great effect to constantly deliver value to our clients. We love to teach and run public training courses and community events to help our industry embedded these practices into their teams.
