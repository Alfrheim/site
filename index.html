Quality

We don't negotiate on quality. Software must deliver more value with time and not be a source of increasing pain and cost. We apply XP Practices like Test-Driven Development, Pair Programming, and Continuous Integration, combined with solid design principles, to ensure quality in all our projects. We continuously invest in our skills, making sure we master all practices we use.

Collaboration

We don't build software alone. Successful software projects are a result of a productive partnership between developers and all stakeholders. Collaboration is at the heart of our development practices. We use Agile processes and Collaborative Development Models to encourage collaboration. We use DDD and BDD to make sure business concepts are collaboratively explored and reflected in our code.

Passion

Delivering quality software isn't just a job for us. It's our passion. We expect nothing less than delighting our clients with the work we do. We are professionals and we don't compromise our values. We strongly believe that technical communities are essential to move our industry forward, which is why we founded and organise the London Software Craftsmanship Community (LSCC), the largest Software Craftsmanship community in the world.
